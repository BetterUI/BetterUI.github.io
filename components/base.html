Almost all of Blaze's styles build on top of base.css. It's a tiny extension of Normalize.css - A modern, HTML5-ready alternative to CSS resets.

Normalize.css makes browsers render all elements more consistently and in line with modern standards. It precisely targets only the styles that need normalizing.
https://necolas.github.io/normalize.css

You do not have to use base.css with all of our modules so give them a try and if you're happy without then it's your call.
