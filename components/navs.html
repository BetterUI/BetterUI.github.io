Fixed positions

.c-nav--top or .c-nav--bottom

Fix the nav to the top or bottom respectively, best used with .c-nav--inline.

.c-nav--left or .c-nav--right

Fix the nav to the left or right respectively, you must apply a width otherwise it will consume the entire page!

Shadows

.c-nav--high, .c-nav--higher and .c-nav--highest

Add these modifiers will give the nav the appearance that it is hovering above the page.
