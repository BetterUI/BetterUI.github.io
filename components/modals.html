--- layout: page title: Modals ---

Modal popups, with great power comes great responsibility. Please use modals wisely.

Basic Modal

Your basic modal has a variety of elements to it. .overlay will expand to fill the current space, more information on overlays is available.

The modal it self consists of a .modal__header, .modal__body and a .modal__footer.

Modal heading

This is the modal body 
<div class="overlay"></div>
<div class="modal">
  <header class="modal__header">
    <button type="button" class="button button--close">×</button>
    <h3 class="heading heading--small">Modal heading</h3>
  </header>

  <div class="modal__body">
    This is the modal body
  </div>

  <footer class="modal__footer">
    <button type="button" class="button button--primary">Close</button>
  </footer>
</div>

Ghost Modal

This modal has no body, hence "ghost". Lol.

To make a ghost modal simply add the .modal--ghost modifier.

Modal heading

This is the modal body 
<div class="overlay"></div>
<div class="modal modal--ghost">
  <header class="modal__header">
    <button type  ="button" class="button button--close">×</button>
    <h3 class="heading heading--small">Modal heading</h3>
  </header>

  <div class="modal__body">
    This is the modal body
  </div>

  <footer class="modal__footer">
    <button type="button" class="button button--primary">Close</button>
  </footer>
</div>

Block footer buttons

Apply the .modal__footer--block modifier to the footer to make your buttons full width.

Modal heading

This is the modal body 
<div class="overlay"></div>
<div class="modal">
  <header class="modal__header">
    <button type  ="button" class="button button--close">×</button>
    <h3 class="heading heading--small">Modal heading</h3>
  </header>

  <div class="modal__body">
    This is the modal body
  </div>

  <footer class="modal__footer modal__footer--block">
    <button type="button" class="button button--success">Save</button>
    <button type="button" class="button button--error">Cancel</button>
  </footer>
</div>
