Slide in menus, but "menu" was already taken so welcome to drawers.

Basic Drawer

Your basic drawer will appear from the bottom of the container. The container should have overflow:hidden or you'll see the drawer sliding around.

To make the drawer appear and disappear toggle the .drawer--visible modifier.
