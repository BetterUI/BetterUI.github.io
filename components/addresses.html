Addresses in Blaze are essentially a reset to a more consistent style:

<address class="address">
  742 Evergreen Terrace,<br>
  Springfield,<br>
  80085,<br>
  USA<br>
</address>
742 Evergreen Terrace,
Springfield,
80085,
USA

You can also use .address__heading to add labels:

<address class="address">
  <span class="address__heading">The Simpsons</span>
  742 Evergreen Terrace,<br>
  Springfield,<br>
  80085,<br>
  USA
</address>
